RIHANNA'S DIAMONDS TOUR!

Diamonds World Tour is the ongoing fifth concert tour by Barbadian recording artist Rihanna. Launched to support her seventh studio album Unapologetic (2012). The tour began on March 8, 2013 in Buffalo, New York.


Rihanna's garments were designed by a variety of designers including Riccardo Tisci of Givenchy, Raf Simons of Christian Dior and Alber Elbaz, footwear was designed by french fashion line Christian Louboutin and Manolo Blahnik. The tour was met with praise from critics who praised Rihanna's stage presence and her vocals " fantastic show and was in excellent voice". Commercially the tour was a success with extra dates being added due to high demand.
