
Censor Ratings explained
Please check suitability for your movie
U

Unrestricted Public Exhibition throughout India, suitable for all age groups
U/A

Unrestricted public exhibition with parental guidance for children below age 12
A

Viewing restricted to adults above 18 years only
S

Exhibition to restricted audience such as doctors etc
Please note that the above ratings are mandated by Central Board of Film Censor and the Cinema Management can deny admissions for non compliance of the same.
